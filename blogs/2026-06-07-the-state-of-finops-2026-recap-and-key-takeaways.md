The 2026 State of FinOps report reveals that artificial intelligence has become central to cloud cost management, with 98% of organizations now tracking AI expenses. While cost optimization remains a primary focus, teams have already captured major savings opportunities and now face incremental improvements requiring greater effort. FinOps has expanded significantly beyond public cloud infrastructure to encompass SaaS, software licensing, private cloud, and data center spending, with organizational leadership increasingly reporting to CTO/CIO executives rather than finance departments.
